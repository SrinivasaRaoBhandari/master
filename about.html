About Us

Masterlog is a  recruitment firm headquartered in Munich, is the youngest subsidiary company of Helvetia Holding PLC in Switzerland

Since 2005 through different subsidiaries, we have continued to successfully deliver temporary staffing search solutions in the logistics industry. We are now expanding our expertise into the Informational Technology sector and are committed to securing exceptional talent for organizations.

We also offer our staffing services and recruitment solutions from our branch offices in Poland, Ukraine, China and India (opening shortly)
